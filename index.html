  </ul>

  <p>The data is used to:</p>

    <li>Improve the overall user experience</li>

  <p>We use <strong>Unity Ads</strong>, provided by Unity Technologies. You can read their full privacy policy here:<br>

  <p>In accordance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, we request your permission to track your device for ad personalization.</p>
