Privacy Policy for Papilio Quest

Last updated: June 3, 2025

1. About this app

Papilio Quest is a mobile game available on the Apple App Store, developed and published by Papillonic Studios ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and this policy explains how we collect and use data.

2. What data do we collect?

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, or contact details.

However, we use third-party services — primarily Unity Ads — which may collect limited device and usage data, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address
  • Device identifiers (e.g. Apple IDFA)
  • In-app activity (e.g. ad views and engagement)

3. Why do we collect this data?

The data is used to:

  • Show ads within the app
  • Measure app performance and ad effectiveness
  • Improve the overall user experience

We use Unity Ads, provided by Unity Technologies. You can read their full privacy policy here:
https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In accordance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, we request your permission to track your device for ad personalization.

If you choose “Allow”, Unity Ads may use the IDFA to show personalized ads.
If you choose “Don’t Allow”, only non-personalized ads will be shown.

You can change your decision at any time via:
Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

5. Data sharing

We do not share any data directly with third parties. However, Unity Ads may process certain data under their own privacy terms.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is intended for general audiences and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please use the app only with parental permission.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Withdraw consent at any time via device settings
  • Delete the app to stop data collection
  • Contact us for more information
